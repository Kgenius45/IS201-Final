The "Triple Slash" and Beyond

In modern dynasty leagues, we look far past basic home runs. Here is how I prioritize data:

  1. Standard Slash Line
    • Batting Average (AVG)
    • On-Base Percentage (OBP)
    • Slugging Percentage (SLG)
  2. Advanced Metrics
    • wRC+ (Weighted Runs Created Plus)
    • Exit Velocity & Hard Hit %
    • Barrel Rate
